Biography

I obtained my Ph.D. in the Department of CIE, the Chinese University of Hong Kong(Shenzhen), under the supervision of Prof. Xiaoguang Han and Prof. Jiangbo Lu. My research interests lie primarily in computer vision and machine learning. I have done some works about 3d human pose and shape estimation, super-resolution, inpainting, video restoration and frame interpolation.
